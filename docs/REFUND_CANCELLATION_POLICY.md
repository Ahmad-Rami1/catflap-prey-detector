This Refund and Cancellation Policy applies to all subscriptions to the Catflap Prey Detector API service (the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully before subscribing.

By subscribing to the Service, you acknowledge and agree to this no-refund policy.

- Billing occurs on the same day each month as your initial subscription date

## 4. Cancellation Policy

### 4.1 How to Cancel
To cancel your subscription, you must send a cancellation request to:

**Email:** florian.mutel[at]gmail.com

**Subject Line:** "Subscription Cancellation Request"

**Required Information:**
- Your registered email address
- Your payment method (last 4 digits)
- Reason for cancellation (optional but appreciated)

### 4.2 Cancellation Processing
- Cancellation requests are processed manually
- We aim to process all cancellation requests within 2-3 business days
- You will receive a confirmation email once your cancellation is processed
- If you do not receive confirmation within 5 business days, please contact us again

### 4.3 Effective Date of Cancellation
- Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period
- You will retain full access to the Service until the end of the paid period
- No prorated refunds will be provided for the remaining days in your billing period
- Your API keys will be deactivated at the end of the billing period

### 4.4 Important Notes
- You are responsible for cancelling before your next billing date if you wish to avoid being charged
- Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 24 hours before your renewal date to guarantee processing before the next charge
- We are not responsible for charges that occur if you fail to cancel in time

## 5. Service Access After Cancellation

### 5.1 During Current Billing Period
After submitting a cancellation request but before the end of your current billing period:
- Your API keys remain active
- You can continue using the Service normally
- All rate limits and quotas still apply

### 5.2 After Billing Period Ends
Once your current billing period expires:
- Your API keys will be deactivated immediately
- API requests will return authentication errors

### 5.3 Reactivation
If you wish to reactivate your subscription after cancellation:
- You may resubscribe at any time
- You will be charged the current subscription rate
- You will receive new API keys
